I am William Ryland, the founder and sole member of Ryland Consulting. When you hire Ryland Consulting, I will personally work with you.

Since graduating from Tulane University with Cum laude honors in English, I have devoted myself to raising funds for worthy non-profit agencies across the country. Fresh out of college, as an in-house grant writer for a small local organization, I was able to increase annual grant revenue by over 50% in two years.

Equipped with successful experience winning grants from government and foundation sources, I moved with my fiancé to California in 2009, where a mid-sized non-profit immediately recognized my gifts and hired me as a grant writer. Again, I succeeded in reaching my goals, this time in the midst of an extremely difficult economic and fundraising environment.

The experience of running the grant writing operations of two very different organizations has prepared me to manage complex grant strategies successfully.

Throughout my career as an in-house grant writer, I also maintained a consistent consulting clientele, securing grants for a wide array of agencies. I have now raised more than $20 million for a broad spectrum of causes, ranging from domestic violence shelters to sea turtle rehabilitation. This broad and deep experience has prepared me to excel as a fundraiser for almost any charitable cause.
